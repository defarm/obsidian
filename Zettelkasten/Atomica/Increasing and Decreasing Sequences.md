### Definition:
A sequence $(s_n)$ is increasing if:
$$ (s_n) < (s_{n+1})\ \forall n \in \mathbb{N}  $$
It is called decreasing if:
$$ (s_n) > (s_{n+1})\ \forall n \in \mathbb{N}  $$
