### Definition:
If $m$ is an [[Upper and Lower Bound|upper bound]] of $S$ and also in $S$, then $m$ is called the maximum of $S$.

If $m$ is a [[Upper and Lower Bound|Lower Bound]] of $S$ and also in $S$, then $m$ is called the minimum of $S$.
