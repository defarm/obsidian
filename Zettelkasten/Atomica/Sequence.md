A **sequence** $s$ is a [[function]] whose [[domain]] is $\mathbb{N}$. However, instead of denoting the value of $s$ at $n$ by $s(n)$, we denote it $s_n$. The [[ordered]] [[set]] of all values of $s$ is denoted $(s_n)$.
