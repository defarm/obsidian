### Definition:
Let $S$ be a [[subset]] of $\mathbb{R}$.

If there exists an $m\in \mathbb{R}$ such that $m\geq s$  for all $s \in S$, then $m$ is called an upper bound of $S$.

If $m \leq s$ for all $s \in S$, then $m$ is called a lower bound of $S$.
