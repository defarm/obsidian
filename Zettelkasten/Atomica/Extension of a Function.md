### Definition:
Suppose $f:(a,b) \to \mathbb{R}$. Then the **extension** of the [[function]] $f$ is called $f^*: [a, b] \to \mathbb{R}$ and is defined as:
$$f^*=\begin{cases}
u & x=a\\ f(x) & a < x < b \\
v & x=b
\end{cases}
$$
where $$\lim_{x\to a} f(x) = u$$ and
$$\lim_{x\to b} f(x) = v$$
