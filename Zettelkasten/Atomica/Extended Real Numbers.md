### Definition:
For convenience, we [[extend]] the [[set]] of [[real numbers]] with $\infty$ and $-\infty$. That is:
$$ \mathbb{R} \cup \{-\infty, \infty\} $$
If the set $S$ is not [[bounded]] above, then we can write:
$$ \sup S = \infty $$
