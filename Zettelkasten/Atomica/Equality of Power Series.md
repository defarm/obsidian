### Insight:
If $\sum_{n=0}^{\infty}a_nx^n=\sum_{n=0}^{\infty}b_nx^n$ for every $n$ in some [[interval]] $(-R, R)$ where $R>0$, then $a_{n}= b_n$ for every $n \in \mathbb{N} \cup \{0\}$.
