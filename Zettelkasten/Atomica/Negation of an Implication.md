### Definition:
The negation of an [[Implication]] read "not $p$ implies $q$" is logically equivalent to the statement $p$ and not $q$. In notation:
$$ \neg (p \implies q)  \iff p \wedge (\neg q)$$
See this in the [[Logic Table|logic table]].
