### Definition:
Let $f: (a,b) \to \mathbb{R}$, then the **right hand limit** of $f$ at $a$ is denoted:
$$ \lim_{ x \to a^+ } f(x) = L $$
and is defined by:
$$ \forall \epsilon > 0,\ \exists \delta >0: a < x < a + \delta \implies |f(x) - L| < \epsilon$$
