### Definition:
If $(s_n)$ is any [[sequence]], and $(n_k)$ is any [[Increasing and Decreasing#^9e95ed|strictly increasing]] sequence, then the sequence $s_{n_{k}}$ is called a subsequence of $(s_n)$.
